Welcome

Welcome to my website! I am an AI researcher and user who focuses on ensemble LLMs.

Ensembles are when you take multiple models and combine their outputs to get better results than any single model could achieve on its own. I am particularly interested in how these models can work together to categorize unstructured text data more effectively.

My research explores the potential of multi-LLM collaboration, where different language models (LLMs) can share insights and improve their performance through collective intelligence. This approach not only enhances the accuracy of text categorization but also opens up new avenues for understanding complex data sets.

In addition to my research, I am also passionate about sharing knowledge and insights with the community. Through this website, I aim to provide updates on my work and also show off my skills and any products I make.
